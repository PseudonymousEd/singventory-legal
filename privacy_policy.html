Singventory Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Singventory ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Singventory (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access or use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Venue Information: Names, locations, and details of karaoke venues you add to the app
  • Song Information: Song titles, artists, key adjustments, and favorite status
  • Performance Data: Records of songs performed at venues, including timestamps
  • Visit Records: Information about your visits to venues

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and performance metrics via Firebase Analytics
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version
  • Log Data: App crashes, errors, and diagnostic information
Important: All venue, song, and performance data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal karaoke data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features like venue management, song tracking, and performance recording
  • App Improvement: Anonymous usage analytics help us understand how users interact with the app
  • Technical Support: Crash reports and error logs help us identify and fix bugs
  • Performance Optimization: Usage patterns help us optimize app performance and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your personal data (venues, songs, performances) is stored locally on your device using encrypted SQLite database
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your personal karaoke data on our servers or in the cloud
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage analytics are processed by Google Firebase Analytics in accordance with Google's privacy policies
  • Data Security: We implement appropriate technical measures to protect data transmitted to analytics services

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • Analytics Services: Anonymous usage data is shared with Google Firebase Analytics for app improvement purposes
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale, or acquisition (users will be notified)

6. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates (Google Privacy Policy)

7. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete individual records or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Export: Data can be backed up through Android's backup system
  • Analytics Opt-out: You can disable analytics in the app settings (feature to be added in future version)

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete such information immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Analytics data may be processed by Google Firebase in various countries. Google provides appropriate safeguards for international data transfers as described in their privacy policy.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date and notify users through the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
